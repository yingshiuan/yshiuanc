    Learn Languages through exploring the world


  • Multi-Multi is an immersive app which assists users with learning languages through attractions around the world. Multi-Multis’ primary target users include children and adults who wants to practice languages and explore the world. Through understanding different cultures and then reduce the language barrier with human.

  • Multi-Multi Language Application

    • Challenge

      The strategy team at UNIMPEDED has identified the understanding of different cultures and exploration of attractions around the world is helpful to practice the languages.

      Solution

      Design an app that will improve learning quality and help people practice the languages in intriguing way.

  • Usability Study

  • I conducted two rounds of usability studies. Findings from the first study helped guide the designs from wireframes to mockups. The second study used a high-fidelity prototype and revealed what aspects of the mockups needed refining.

    • Insight

      Basic learning materials


      People need materials about basics conversation or how to start a small talk.

      Search function


      People start to search the attraction they want to visit and plan their trip.

      Add to favorites


      People want to add different categories to the collection as their notes and also can practice languages and prepare conversations for their trips.
