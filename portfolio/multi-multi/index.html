  • Multi-Multi is an immersive app which assists users with learning languages through attractions around the world. Through understanding different cultures and then reduce the language barrier with human.

  • Multi-Multi Language Application

    • Challenge

      The strategy team at UNIMPEDED has identified the understanding of different cultures and exploration of attractions around the world is helpful to practice the languages.

      Solution

      Design an app that will improve learning quality and help people practice the languages in intriguing way.

  • Research

    • The Product's Goal

      Learning the languages should be fun and immersive in the environment to keep motivation high.

  • Persona

      “I want to explore more new things in my life, I feels good when I learned more Chinese.”

      “My routine is very regular, taking care of my family and try something new during my free time.”
