    Robotic Fabrication and Clay material


  • This project focuses on the study of robotic fabrication processes for malleable materials, where the precise control of forces and positions are paramount. Through iterations of empiric testing, design simulation and physical prototyping, different typologies of the technique are explored. Here we aim on increasing the interlocking behavior of the projectiles of clay material that leads to better structural performance on surfaces with curvature.
