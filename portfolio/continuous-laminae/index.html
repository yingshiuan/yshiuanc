    Laminae

    Abstract Machine for the study of Materiality


  • The project starts from researching materiality of composite wooden materials. We develop and design four different concept models based on materiality. Furthermore, we design four architectural tectonics in parametric a way. The design process spans computational modelling in Rhino3D all the way to digital fabrication with machines, e.g. laser cutters, 3D-printers, CNC etc. The goal of this project is to achieve quicker iterations in the design process from digital to physical.
