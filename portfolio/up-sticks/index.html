    Hello Robot Exhibition - V&A Dundee Museum


  • Our project, Up-Sticks, is an expressive timber structure assembled by spruce wood planks and beech wood dowels. This project allows me to gain more experience in the field of collaborative robotic fabrication.


  • Collaborative Robotic Fabrication

    Computational Design

  • V&A Dundee - Hello Robot
    Up Sticks

  • I enjoy exploring the flexibility and ease of access of robots within the field of computational design and robotic fabrication. I also enjoy expanding my understanding of the robotic configuration space and its constraints.

    With my experience of in the field of robotic fabrication, I provide suggestions regarding the assembly sequence to our team.

  • Fabrication Process

  • By adding strategically placed staying points along the robotic path, we ensure that each element in each module is always reachable by the robotic arm in the following situations:

    1. In one continuous motion, the robotic arm picks up an element and places it.
    2. After the robotic arm places the previous element, it moves to pick up the next element.

  • Sequence

  • We then orient 16 modules on fabrication platform to ensure that each element in each module is always reachable by the robotic arm.

  • The fabrication setup and environment takes advantage of the facilities available in the Robotic Fabrication Laboratory and the research environment at ETH Zurich.
