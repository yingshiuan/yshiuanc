  • Frame
    Möbel


  • Frame Möbel is a creative project exploring the possibilities of spatial frames in furniture design. The project aims to develop form-finding algorithms for space frame table design and parametrically explore the formal and aesthetic possibilities of additively manufactured joints.

      In this project, I simulated lightweight structures in the 3D modeling software, Rhinoceros 3D. Prototyped customized joints by 3D-printing.
