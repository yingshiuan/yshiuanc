    Meal Subscription Boxes Responsive Website


  • Foo:D is a Meal Subscription Boxes that offers affordable pricing options. The typical user is between 25-45 years old, and most users are busy or enjoy cooking. Foo:D’s goal is to make cooking fun, fast, and easy for all types of users.

  • Foo:D

    • Challenge

      Available online ordering meal websites have cluttered designs, inefficient systems for browsing through products, and confusing checkout processes.

      Solution

      Design a Foo:D website to be user friendly by providing clear navigation and offering a fast checkout process.

  • Research

    • The Product's Goal

      Design a Foo:D website to be accessible by providing clear navigation and offering a fast checkout process.
