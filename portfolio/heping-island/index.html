  • Proposal for Shipbuilding and Archeology

  • This project aims to focus on the problems and potential of Heping Island in Keelung City, which is suffering from such urban decay despite its picturesque environment, historical significance, and proximity to a major container port.

    In order to address this problem, we will critically explore this theme and develop a deeper understanding of the natural resources, manufactured goods, infrastructure networks, and natural climatic elements that are found in the increasingly post-industrial landscape of Taiwan and define what are the inputs and outputs of these industries.
