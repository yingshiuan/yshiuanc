  • Robotic
    Drawing


  • Robotic drawing is the process of converting human drawing techniques into robotic arms. We mounted a custom tool to hold the pen as the end effector for the robotic arm (UR5) and calibrated it to draw on paper.


  • Computational Design

      Role & Activities

      I designed the graphics using the accuracy of the robotic arm and set up the configuration of the robotic arm to draw in the correct location.
  • Robotic Drawing

  • The drawing path of the UR5 is generated by the graphics, and the graphics are designed on the Grasshopper plugin Kangaroo. Kangaroo is a real-time physics engine for interactive simulation, form-finding, optimization, and constraint solving.
